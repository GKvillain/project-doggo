Guide to the World of Dogs.

Early humans and wolves likely developed a mutual relationship: wolves scavenged near camps, while humans benefited from their alertness and hunting skills.

Over 30,000 years ago

20,000-15,000 years ago

Archaeological evidence shows humans began taming and selectively breeding wolves. Dogs became companions, guards, and hunting partners.

In ancient civilizations, dogs spread worldwide with humans, serving as hunters, protectors, and companions, with roles in Egypt, Mesopotamia, and China shaping their importance in society.

10,000-2,000 years ago

2,000 years ago - 1800s

Different roles led to specialized breeds such as Hunting dogs (retrievers), Guard dogs (mastiffs), Companion dogs (toy breeds) Herding dogs (sheepdogs)

Dogs serve as companions, service animals, therapy dogs, and working partners.Genetic studies confirm all dogs trace back to wolves, yet they've evolved to live alongside humans more closely than any other species.

1800s - Today
