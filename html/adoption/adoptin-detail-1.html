Hi, I'm Dokdak

  • Age: 7 years and 8 months
  • Gender: Female
  • Health: Vaccinated, neutered, healthy
  • Size: Medium(about 18kg)
  • Medical history: No chronic illness, regular checkups completed
  • Temperament: Affectionate, playful, loyal
  • Energy Level: Moderate - enjoys walks, play, and rest
  • Compatibility: Good with kids and dogs
  • Personality: Curious and loves exploring
  • Special Quirks: Belly rubs, follows people, head tilt
  • House Training: Fully trained, keeps routine
  • Commands: Knows sit, stay, paw
  • Leash Walking: Walks well, pulls when excited
  • Socialization: Friendly with people and dogs
  • Needs Improvement: Shy with loud noise/crowds

Dokdak is a sweet and playful girl who loves belly rubs and going for walks. Rescued from the streets, she has been waiting patiently for her forever home. She's friendly with kids and gets along well with other dogs, making her a perfect family companion. Curious and active, Dokdak enjoys exploring new places and would be happiest with a family who can give her both love and space to run around. Despite her energy, she has a gentle side and adores quiet cuddle time after play. With her kind nature and loyal heart, Dokdak is ready to bring joy and companionship to a loving home.

How to Adopt

Browse Profiles

Look through the available dogs on our website. Each profile includes details about the dog and the foundation currently caring for them.

Contact the Foundation

If you find a dog you're interested in, please use the contact details provided in their profile. Reach out directly to the foundation for more information.

Application & Interview

The foundation will guide you through their adoption application. They may ask questions to make sure the dog is a good match for your lifestyle.

Meet & Greet

You'll usually have the chance to meet the dog in person at the foundation or in a foster home.

Home Check & Approval

Some foundations may do a home visit or request photos to ensure a safe environment for the dog.

Adoption Fee & Agreement

If approved, you'll sign an adoption agreement and pay a small fee (which supports the foundation's rescue work).
